About COSTECH

The Tanzania Commission for Science and Technology (COSTECH) is a premier government institution tasked with coordinating and promoting science, technology, and innovation in Tanzania. Established to drive the country's technological advancements, COSTECH fosters research, innovation, and development initiatives that contribute to national progress.

Our Values and Ethics

At COSTECH, we uphold the highest standards of integrity, professionalism, and ethical conduct in all our operations. We are committed to creating an inclusive and respectful environment for all employees, interns, and partners. Our core values include innovation, transparency, accountability, and collaboration, ensuring that we contribute positively to Tanzania's scientific and technological landscape.

  • Integrity: Acting with honesty and integrity in all interactions.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creative and forward-thinking solutions.
  • Collaboration: Fostering teamwork and open communication.
  • Respect: Promoting a culture of mutual respect and diversity.
  • Accountability: Holding ourselves accountable for our actions and decisions.
